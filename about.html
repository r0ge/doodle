Meet Cissy Saltarelli, the face behind Doodle Lew Macramé. A native Californian, Cissy grew up in San Francisco, moved to Chicago for a time. She currently lives in Orange County, California. Cissy began her macramé journey in the 1970's when she taught her beloved mother how to make plant hangers. Recently, her creative eye for design led her back to her passion for macramé. The name Doodle Lew Macramé comes from finding many finished antler and driftwood macramé pieces outside, being chewed on by the family’s golden doodle, Lewis.
