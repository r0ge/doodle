Meet Cissy Saltarelli, the face behind Doodle Lew Macramé. Cissy began her macramé journey in the 1970's when she taught her beloved mother how to make plant hangers. Recently, her creative eye for design led her back to her passion for macramé.

Macramé is a textile art form that uses various materials and patterned knots to create beautiful home decor. Each piece of Doodle Lew Macramé is uniquely designed and handmade. The materials used are all high-quality and often handmade, vintage, or limited in quantity, making each creation a treasure. To inquire about the availability of a specific piece, please contact us at doodlelewmacrame@gmail.com. Each piece is made to order and may differ slightly from the photo, giving it character, because it will be handmade for you.

The name Doodle Lew Macramé comes from finding many finished antler and driftwood macramé pieces outside, being chewed on by the family’s golden doodle, Lewis.
